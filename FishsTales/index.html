Fish's Tale!

You can find the app I have spent most of quarantine developing by searching "Fish's Tale" on the Apple App Store or by clicking this link. It is a complex app. The main idea is to track where fish are most often caught by recording data. Using the app, you can easily discover which spot or lure or water depth works the best for any fishing hole. I am super proud of it! I made it for my older brother who loves to fish.
