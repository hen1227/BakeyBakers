BakeyBakers life

I first started when I wanted a bake sale, but I thought our fold-out tables were to dirty. My mom offered if I gave her the next two hundred dollars I make from the Bake Sale She would make me a cart that I could use. On my first Bake Sale, I thought it was fun so I started calling it bakey bakers, more as a joke, but it stuck now I own a website called BakeyBakers.com
